A little look around Morocco

Morocco is a wonderful country, full of very nice people and wonderful landscapes!
One of my best meeting over there, was a family that invited me to their home and even if they did not have very much, they were full of love. They painted my hands with henna, offered me tea and dinner, wanted to show me their neighborhood and neighbors, ...
I can say that Morocco is a country full of surprises!

Travelling through Europe

Travelling in Europe is so much fun! You always meet other backpackers ( that you might see later on on your road by pure luck!) and there is always so much things to see!
Between beautiful Sevilla in Spain, the interesting capital of Albania, Tirana; the cold but amazing Warsaw in Poland, wonderful Portugal and Italy, you never get bored to discover new places !

A little detour by Mexico!

I had a wonderfull in Mexico! Between the mountains, the jungle, the city, the beach, the desert,..., you never have enough time to visit everything!
One of my favorite thing in Mexico is ... the FOOD! Delicious tacos, fresh avocados, all the different type of salsa that you can find, the strange but wonderfull candies...
